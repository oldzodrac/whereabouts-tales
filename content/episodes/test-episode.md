# The Mystery of the Alps

Deep in the heart of the European continent lies a range of mountains that have guarded their secrets for millennia. The Alps are not just a geological wonder; they are a repository of human history, myth, and unexplained phenomena.

## The Whispering Peaks

Locals often speak of the "Whispering Peaks," where the wind carries voices of the past. Some say it's just the acoustics of the valleys, but others believe the mountains themselves are alive, documenting every traveler who dares to cross their paths.

### What We Found

During our last expedition, we discovered a series of carvings in a cave near the Eiger. These carvings don't match any known historical records of the region. They depict celestial events that shouldn't have been visible from this latitude at the time they were made.
